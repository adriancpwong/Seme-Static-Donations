With our society growing more and more cashless, our rough sleepers have been made to face several of its consequences. These can easily be solved through using contactless technology for their benefit, and not allowing for their community to be left behind because of limitations in digitalism.

While some are normally unable to have a personal bank account, SE|ME allows for your donations to go directly onto a beneficiary's personal ‘ME’ card. A card which is redeemed in an autonomous manner in any of our partner retailers, of the beneficiary's own choice.

Find out the impact that you could be making through SE|ME

Find out more about how the SEME pathway is helping rough sleepers through facilitating a growth in personal ability, granting them autonomy and by providing them wraparound support.

Use your phone to make a contactless donation to your chosen beneficiary

Scan QR code from placard, signpost or notice board, using your iPhone camera or android QR scanner. You will be redirected to our payment page where you can choose how much to donate to your chosen beneficiary.

You've just increased the balance on someone's ME-card!

You've just helped one of our rough sleepers through allowing them to purchase more food, clothes or other personal items. At the same time as you have given them incentive to take part in our wraparound support system. Thank you!
